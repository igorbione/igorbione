## About me

I'm a **Data Scientist at WAM Group** with an unusual path into the field. I started in scientific research — a **PhD in Ecology & Evolution (UFG)**, with a research period at **Università di Napoli Federico II** — applying machine learning, computer simulation, and causal inference to hard modeling problems.

Today I bring that same methodological rigor to business: I build **forecasting systems, recommendation engines, and applied-AI products that run in production** and move measurable numbers. Currently focused on **LLM applications, AI engineering, and automation** in real-world business contexts.

> I like translating business pain into data problems — end to end, from framing the hypothesis to shipping and monitoring the model.

## Impact highlights

- 📉 **–45% waste** — Hybrid **LSTM + Prophet** forecasting model for F&B consumption across hotel warehouses, generating automated purchase suggestions. In production on **Databricks** with drift monitoring, guardrails, and **MLflow** experiment tracking.
- 📈 **+25% on key products** — Market-basket recommendation system (**Apriori**) from bar/restaurant transactions, helping managers design product combos and grow F&B revenue.
- 🤖 **Applied GenAI** — Conversational AI agents orchestrated with **LangChain / n8n** for customer service and internal-process automation, including work on a sales-focused AI squad with emphasis on security, governance, and ethical use.
- ⏱️ **100% reports approved** — Automated technical-report pipeline (**Python + Markdown + LaTeX**) covering 40+ parameters with an out-of-range alert system, sharply cutting production time with zero rework.
